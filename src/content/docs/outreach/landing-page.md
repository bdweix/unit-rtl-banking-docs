The marketing landing page is a **customizable external page** that showcases your banking product and its benefits to prospective customers — a ready-made top-of-funnel destination you can point campaigns at before customers enter onboarding.

## What it's for

- A **public** page (outside your authenticated app) that explains the banking offering.
- A clear call-to-action into your [onboarding flow](/build/embed-the-app/).
- A fast way to start acquiring before you invest in custom marketing pages.

## Customization

- **Brand it** to match your [theme](/build/theming/) — logo, colors, copy.
- Highlight the benefits that matter to *your* audience (faster payouts, integrated finances, rewards, etc.).
- Wire the CTA to your onboarding entry point.

## Where it fits in the funnel

```
   Campaign / referral ──► Marketing landing page ──► Onboarding (the embed) ──► Funded account
        (ads, email)            (this asset)            unit-elements-…           book payments
```

:::note
The landing page component, hosting model, and the extent of customization are Unit-provided and vary by account. Confirm how to deploy and brand it — and whether it's hosted by Unit or embedded by you — with your solution engineer.
:::
