Marketing banners are ready-to-use UI you embed in **your** product to promote the banking offering and nudge customers toward opening and funding an account.

## Formats

| Format | Where it fits | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Promo cards** | Inline in dashboards, feeds, or settings. | Introduce banking; click-through to onboarding. |
| **Sidebar recommendations** | Persistent nav / sidebar. | Steady, low-friction discovery. |
| **Hero banners** | Top of a key page. | High-visibility launch push or campaign. |

## How to use them

1. Place the banner where your customers naturally make money decisions (billing, payouts, dashboards).
2. Link the call-to-action to your banking page (the one hosting the [embed](/build/embed-the-app/)).
3. Match the banner styling to your [theme](/build/theming/) for a native feel.
4. Measure click-through and funding rate so you can iterate placement.

:::tip
The highest-converting placement is usually wherever the customer already thinks about money in your product. A "get paid faster" promo next to a payouts screen beats a generic banner on a home page.
:::

:::note
Exact banner components, props, and asset sources are provided by Unit and may vary by account. Confirm what's available and how to embed each format with your solution engineer, then wire the CTAs to your onboarding entry point.
:::
