Unit can send **automated, branded lifecycle emails** on your behalf to guide customers through activation and keep them engaged — without you building an email program for banking.

## The lifecycle these cover

| Stage | Purpose of the email |
| --- | --- |
| **Activation** | Nudge a customer who started but hasn't finished onboarding, or hasn't funded. |
| **Onboarding outcomes** | Communicate approval / next steps. |
| **Engagement** | Encourage ongoing use (cards, deposits, transfers). |

The emails are branded to you (logo, colors, sender identity) and sent by Unit so they stay consistent with the banking experience.

## Working with them

- **Branding:** align the email branding with your [theme](/build/theming/) and brand assets.
- **Triggers:** sends are driven by customer state (e.g. incomplete onboarding). Confirm the exact trigger set with Unit.
- **Coordination:** if you *also* email customers about banking, coordinate so messages don't overlap or contradict.

:::note
The exact template set, trigger conditions, and customization options are Unit-provided and can vary by account. Get the current catalog and any opt-in/opt-out controls from your solution engineer.
:::
