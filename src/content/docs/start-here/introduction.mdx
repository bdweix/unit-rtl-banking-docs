Ready-to-Launch (RtL) Banking is a **pre-built, white-labeled banking application** you embed in your product. Unit hosts the UI, runs onboarding (KYC/KYB), manages compliance and fraud, and operates the underlying bank accounts. You provide the customer, the brand, and a place to render one web component.

The trade is simple: you give up pixel-level control over the UI in exchange for shipping a real, compliant banking product in weeks instead of quarters.

:::tip[The one-sentence mental model]
You authenticate your user, mint a **JWT**, and hand it to a **web component**. Unit renders the entire banking experience inside that component and tells you what's happening through **webhooks** and a small read-oriented **HTTP API**.
:::

## Who this is for

Ready-to-Launch is a good fit when:

- You want embedded banking as a **feature inside an existing product**, not a standalone neobank.
- You want to **validate demand** or get to market quickly before investing in a custom build.
- You have **one engineer for ~1–2 weeks** and don't want to build banking UI, onboarding, or compliance workflows yourself.
- You're comfortable with Unit's hosted UI and the customization that theming provides.

It's *not* the right fit if you need a fully bespoke UI, deep programmatic control over every banking primitive, or advanced risk/fraud tooling on day one. For that, see [Custom vs. Ready-to-Launch](/start-here/custom-vs-ready-to-launch/).

## What's included

| Capability | What you get |
| --- | --- |
| **Onboarding** | Hosted KYC (individuals, sole props) and KYB (businesses) application flow. |
| **Deposit accounts** | Interest-bearing checking accounts with routing/account numbers and balances. |
| **Cards** | Physical and virtual debit cards with programmatic controls. |
| **Payments** | ACH, same-day ACH, wire, check, and free instant 24/7 book transfers. |
| **Money-in** | Mobile check deposit and fee-free nationwide ATM access. |
| **Teams** | Owner / Admin / ReadOnly / Cardholder roles for business accounts. |
| **Integrations** | Plaid and Intuit QuickBooks. |
| **Outreach** | Branded emails, marketing banners, and a landing page component. |

Compliance, risk, fraud ownership, and end-user banking support are **managed by Unit**.

## The five things you'll integrate

<Steps>

1. **The embed.** A `<unit-elements-white-label-app>` web component (or native SDK) that renders the whole experience. See [Embed the app](/build/embed-the-app/).

2. **Authentication.** A JWT minted from your identity provider that identifies the end-user to Unit. See [Authentication & JWT](/build/authentication/).

3. **Branding.** Colors, logo, fonts, and card art via the dashboard or a `settings-json` attribute. See [Theming & branding](/build/theming/).

4. **Webhooks.** Server-to-server events for applications, accounts, cards, and transactions. See [Webhooks](/operate/webhooks/).

5. **The API.** A read-oriented HTTP API for accounts, customers, and transactions, plus book payments. See [API overview](/api/overview/).

</Steps>

Everything beyond these — pre-fill, multi-user, unified onboarding with Stripe, end-user events — is **optional** and layered on as you need it.
